## 6. Stakeholder Risk Flags

| Flag ID | Description | Trigger | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| SR-01 | Regulatory approval confidence drops | >2 permit resubmissions in one quarter | Deploy dedicated permit task force |
| SR-02 | Community sentiment turns negative | >5 formal complaints in one month | Activate community action plan and adjust logistics hours |
| SR-03 | Contractor relationship strain | SPI < 0.90 for 2 consecutive periods | Executive-to-executive recovery workshop |

## 7. Review and Update Rules

- Register is reviewed at least **monthly**.
- Influence/interest scores are recalibrated at each major phase gate.
- Any stakeholder with support level marked **Resistant** must have a corrective engagement action within 10 business days.
