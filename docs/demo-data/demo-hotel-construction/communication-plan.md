# Communications Management Plan

## Governance Cadence

- Daily contractor coordination during peak construction
- Weekly project controls review covering schedule, cost, RAID, and procurement
- Monthly SteerCo pack and decision forum

## Communication Matrix

| Audience | Information | Frequency | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Sponsor Board | Executive status, decisions, variance summary | Monthly | Project Director |
| Site Delivery Team | Look-ahead, constraints, safety escalations | Weekly | Construction Manager |
| Operating Partner | Opening readiness, FF&E, commissioning updates | Bi-weekly | Operations Lead |
| Internal Audit | Control evidence and approval records | Monthly | PMO Governance Lead |

## Escalation Triggers

- SPI below 0.95
- CPI below 0.95
- Any critical safety or compliance issue
- Any change request above USD 250k

## Communication Principles

- Provide one version of the truth for schedule, budget, and risks.
- Escalate early when authority inspections or long-lead packages threaten milestones.
- Keep operating-readiness stakeholders informed before construction close-out.
