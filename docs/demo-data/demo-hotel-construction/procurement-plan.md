## 2. Procurement Objectives

1. Secure qualified suppliers aligned to scope, quality, and schedule.
2. Lock pricing for high-volatility categories early where beneficial.
3. Mitigate long-lead delivery risks for critical equipment.
4. Ensure transparent tendering and governance compliance.

## 5. Source Selection Criteria

| Criterion | Weight |
|---|---:|
| Technical compliance and methodology | 35% |
| Relevant hospitality project references | 20% |
| Commercial competitiveness | 25% |
| Schedule and logistics capability | 10% |
| HSE and quality performance history | 10% |

Minimum technical threshold for commercial opening: **75/100**.

## 6. Procurement Governance and Approvals

| Decision | Approval Authority |
|---|---|
| Tender list approval | Procurement Committee |
| Bid evaluation recommendation | Evaluation Panel + PMO |
| Award up to USD 5M | Project Director |
| Award above USD 5M | Steering Committee |
| Single-source exception | CFO + Sponsor approval |

## 7. Key Procurement Risks and Mitigation

| Risk ID | Description | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| PR-01 | Supplier capacity constraints | Delayed fabrication | Dual sourcing where feasible; early reservation slots |
| PR-02 | FX volatility for imported FF&E | Cost escalation | Hedge policy + currency-adjusted contingencies |
| PR-03 | Logistics/customs bottlenecks | Schedule delay | Dedicated customs broker + pre-clearance documentation |
| PR-04 | Non-compliant substitutions | Quality and warranty risk | Strict submittal workflow and approved vendor list |

## 9. Vendor Performance Management

Monthly vendor scorecard dimensions:

- On-time delivery performance
- Quality defect rate
- NCR closure cycle time
- Safety compliance score
- Payment documentation accuracy

Vendors under 70/100 for two consecutive months are escalated to corrective action.

## 10. Documentation and Audit Trail

- All tender artifacts archived in project data room.
- Bid clarification logs maintained with timestamp and owner.
- Contract change events cross-referenced in the Change Control Log.
- Compliance checks aligned with internal audit requirements.
