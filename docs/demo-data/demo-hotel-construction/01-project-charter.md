## 2. Purpose and Authorization

This charter formally authorizes the **Azure Bay Hotel & Convention Center Project** and assigns authority to the Project Director to plan and execute work within the approved baseline constraints.

The project will deliver a 220-room upscale hotel, a multi-purpose conference center, wellness areas, and associated infrastructure in La Goulette, Tunis.

## 3. Business Case and Strategic Alignment

The project supports the sponsor's 5-year growth strategy to:

1. Increase premium room capacity in Greater Tunis.
2. Capture business tourism through conference and event facilities.
3. Improve year-round occupancy with a mixed leisure/business offer.
4. Strengthen long-term recurring revenue from hospitality operations.

## 5. High-Level Scope

### In Scope

- Site preparation, earthworks, and utility tie-ins.
- Full civil/structural construction of hotel and conference blocks.
- Mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and fire/life safety systems.
- Interior fit-out for guestrooms, lobby, back-of-house, and F&B zones.
- FF&E procurement and installation.
- IT, low current, access control, CCTV, and network backbone.
- Testing, commissioning, integrated systems validation, and handover.

### Out of Scope

- Post-opening hotel operations management.
- Long-term brand marketing campaigns.
- Expansion phase (future second tower concept).

## 8. Key Assumptions and Constraints

### Assumptions

- A-01: Core permits are obtained by 2026-05-20.
- A-02: Long-lead MEP and elevator components are ordered by 2026-06-15.
- A-03: Sponsor payment certifications are processed within 15 calendar days.

### Constraints

- C-01: Peak construction noise restricted in adjacent zone between 22:00 and 06:00.
- C-02: Coastal corrosion requirements increase material and QA standards.
- C-03: Hotel opening date linked to annual business tourism season.

## 10. Governance and Decision Rights

| Forum | Chair | Frequency | Key Decisions |
|---|---|---|---|
| Steering Committee | Executive Sponsor | Monthly | Strategic direction, reserve usage, major escalations |
| Project Control Board | Project Director | Weekly | Schedule/cost/risk performance, corrective actions |
| Change Control Board (CCB) | PMO Director | Bi-weekly | Scope/cost/schedule change approvals |
| Site HSE Committee | HSE Manager | Weekly | Safety actions and compliance status |

Authority levels:

- Project Director may approve changes up to **USD 150,000** and **<= 5 calendar days** impact.
- CCB approval required above this threshold.
- Steering Committee approval required for baseline re-plan or reserve drawdown > USD 500,000.

## 11. Success Criteria and Acceptance

Project success is declared when all conditions are met:

1. Handover package accepted by sponsor and operator.
2. Occupancy and safety certificates obtained.
3. Final cost variance within approved thresholds.
4. No critical defects blocking soft opening.
